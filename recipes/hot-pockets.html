Hot Pockets

About Hot Pockets:

So, there are instructions provided on how to cook hotpockets on the packet. If you haven't thrown that away yet, we recommend you to follow that instead. It's just more convenient for you that way. But lets say that you did... then we got you covered! Here is how you make Hot Pockets

Ingredients:

  • Hot Pockets

Steps:

  1. Get your Hot Pockets out of the box
  2. Put it on a plate
  3. Put it in the microwave
  4. Cover it with a plastic container (so that if it blows up it doesn't mess up the microwave)
  5. Set it for 2 minutes
  6. Take it out and enjoy! :)

Cereal

About Cereal:

Cereal is one of the most basic things you can make for yourself, so much so that many people don't even consider it cooking to make cereal! If you have no idea how to make anything, you got to start somewhere, and cereal is definitely a start. It may not be cooking, but being able to make something is a lot better than nothing.

Ingredients:

  • Milk (or Dried Milk with Water)
  • Pre-made cereal of your choice
  • Optional: add-ons like mini-marshmellows or chocolate chips... if they mix with your particular cereal

Steps:

  1. Get a bowl big enough for your consumption needs
  2. Pour your cereal of choice into the bowl
  3. Pour milk into the bowl (Dont pour milk first. If you do, you are a monster)
  4. Get a spoon that fits your needs
  5. Optional: sprinkle mini-marshmellows or chocolate chips to add some extra flavor
  6. Bring the food to the table and enjoy!

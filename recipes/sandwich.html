Sandwich

About Sandwiches:

Sandwiches are amazing to eat, and frankly, there are a million and a half ways to make one. Since this increadibly official website is designed to help the simplest of simpletons learn the basic of cooking, we're going to keep it as simple as we can :)

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Balogne
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Optional: Lettuce
  • Optional: Tomatoes
  • Optional: Cucumbers
  • (Honestly, anything you may possibly want to add to it, you can)

Steps:

  1. If your bread isn't sliced already, cut two loafs of bread
  2. If you like your bread toasted, put your bread in the toaster
  3. Get your bread on a plate and put your toppings on one of the slices, like a pile
  4. Put the second bread on top
  5. Enjoy! :)
